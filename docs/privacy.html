Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Line5 Orbs ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by Ge Liu. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not require accounts, registration, or login to play. The App does not directly collect or store any personal data on its own servers.

Game Center

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your Game Center display name and scores are handled according to Apple's privacy policy. The App does not access or store your Game Center credentials.

Advertising

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data and ad interaction data

You may be prompted to allow or deny ad tracking via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to permanently remove all advertisements. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The App does not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any first-party analytics or tracking. Third-party ad services may collect anonymized usage data as described in the Advertising section above.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App contains no external links within gameplay.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at GitHub.
