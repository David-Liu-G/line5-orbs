Line5 Orbs

Line up 5 glowing orbs to clear them — how long can you survive?

A classic puzzle strategy game with a magical anime-inspired aesthetic. On an 8×8 grid, 3 colorful glowing orbs appear each turn. Tap to select, tap to move — line up 5 or more same-color orbs to clear them and score points. Plan ahead, think strategically, and see how high you can score before the board fills up!
